Our Story

Austin and Macy met on September 26th 2023. A chance meeting at the University of Oklahoma resulted in a first date, a second, and then many more. After a year at OU, Macy needed to leave Oklahoma to persue better opportunities. They took this leap of faith together and moved to Durango, Colorado. Since then, they both have enjoyed exploring the beautiful nature of the Rocky Mountains and broader southwest region.

After going on two years together, Austin took Macy on a road trip she had always dreamed of. On a secluded beach of the Oregon coast, Austin asked Macy to spend the rest of their lives together.

Now a new chapter of Austin and Macy's lives is beggining in the heart of the San Juan Mountains. They cannot wait to celebrate their love with friends and family at Lake City!
