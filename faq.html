Frequently Asked Questions

  • Am I allowed a plus one?

    • Due to limited venue capacity, we are only able to accommodate the guests listed on your invitation.

    -----

  • Can I bring my children?

    • We love your little ones! However due to the capacity limits of our small venue, we ask that you bring only your children if your envelope was addressed to your whole family or if your children were listed on the envelope.

    -----

  • When should I arrive?

    • The Ceremony will promptly begin at 4:30 PM. We recommend you arrive no later than 4:15 PM to ensure everyone makes it to their seats.

    -----

  • Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

    • Our ceremony and reception will both be outdoors! There will be a large covered area for guests during the reception in case of rain. We recommend wearing shoes comfortable for walking on grass and uneven ground.

    -----

  • What will the weather be like?

    • Expect classic Colorado Mountain weather, which is sunny and mild during the day, but cool once the sun goes down. We recommend bringing layers and jackets for the evening!

    -----

  • Is the venue at high elevation?

    • Yes! Lake City sits around 8,700 feet above sea level. For guests traveling from lower elevations, we recommened staying hydrated and limiting immediate alcohol intake upon arrival.

    -----

  • When should I RSVP by?

    • We kindly ask that you RSVP as soon as you can for our planning. However, the deadline to RSVP is March 6th. If you do not respond by then, we will assume you cannot celebrate with us.
